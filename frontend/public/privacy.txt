PRIVACY POLICY AND DATA DELETION

User data is collected only for basic functionality such as account login and content interaction.

If you choose to log in using Facebook, only basic profile information permitted by Google, Facebook (such as name, profile picture, and email) is used for authentication. No data is sold or shared with third parties.

Data is stored securely and kept only while your account is active.

DATA DELETION

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting the site owner or by deleting your account if that option is available.

All data related to your account (including Google and Facebook login data), will be permanently deleted.
